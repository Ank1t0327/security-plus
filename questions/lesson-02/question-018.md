# L02-Q018

**Difficulty:** Medium
**Domain:** Attack Surface
**Objective:** 3.4 Explain message-based attack vectors
**Security+ Objective Mapping:** SY0-701 Objective 3.4 - Email and messaging attack surfaces

## Scenario
A company is hit by an attack that starts with a text message containing a malicious link to a fake login page. What vector is this?

## Choices
A. Network vector
B. Message-based vector
C. Supply chain vector
D. Physical vector

## Correct Answer
B

## Detailed Explanation
A malicious SMS message is a message-based vector, similar to phishing via email.

## Why the Incorrect Options Are Wrong
- A: This attack uses messaging, not direct network exploitation.
- C: Supply chain is not involved in the SMS lure.
- D: Physical vector is unrelated to SMS messages.

## Exam Tip
Message-based vectors include phishing via email, SMS, and instant messaging.
