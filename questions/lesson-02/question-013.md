# L02-Q013

**Difficulty:** Medium
**Domain:** Attack Surface
**Objective:** 3.3 Explain lure-based attack vectors
**Security+ Objective Mapping:** SY0-701 Objective 3.3 - Lure-based attack surfaces

## Scenario
An employee finds a USB drive labeled "Salary Bonuses" in the parking lot and inserts it into their workstation. What type of attack surface is this?

## Choices
A. Lure-based vector
B. Supply chain vector
C. Network vector
D. Message-based vector

## Correct Answer
A

## Detailed Explanation
This is a lure-based vector: a physical bait designed to entice the victim into executing malicious content.

## Why the Incorrect Options Are Wrong
- B: This is not a supply chain issue.
- C: It is not a network-based attack.
- D: No message or email is involved.

## Exam Tip
Lure-based attacks use enticing items like USB drives or offers to trigger user interaction.
