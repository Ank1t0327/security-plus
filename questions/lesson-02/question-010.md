# L02-Q010

**Difficulty:** Medium
**Domain:** Internal Threats
**Objective:** 2.7 Describe internal threat actors and risks
**Security+ Objective Mapping:** SY0-701 Objective 2.7 - Insider threats and controls

## Scenario
A departing employee copies customer lists and emails a personal account, claiming they need the data for a job search. Which type of threat actor is this?

## Choices
A. Malicious insider
B. Hacktivist
C. Nation-state agent
D. Social engineer

## Correct Answer
A

## Detailed Explanation
A departing employee misusing access for personal gain fits the malicious insider category.

## Why the Incorrect Options Are Wrong
- B: There is no ideological motivation.
- C: No foreign state sponsorship is present.
- D: This is not a social engineering attack by an external actor.

## Exam Tip
Internal threats come from employees or contractors with legitimate access who abuse it.
