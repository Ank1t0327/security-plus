# L02-Q023

**Difficulty:** Medium
**Domain:** Social Engineering
**Objective:** 4.3 Explain phishing and pharming
**Security+ Objective Mapping:** SY0-701 Objective 4.3 - Phishing and pharming techniques

## Scenario
A user receives an email from what appears to be their bank asking them to click a link and confirm account details. Which attack is this?

## Choices
A. Pharming
B. Phishing
C. Typosquatting
D. Vishing

## Correct Answer
B

## Detailed Explanation
This is phishing: a deceptive email guiding the user to a fraudulent site to capture credentials.

## Why the Incorrect Options Are Wrong
- A: Pharming involves DNS redirection or corrupted hosts, not just a malicious email link.
- C: Typosquatting depends on misspelled domains, not the email lure itself.
- D: Vishing is voice phishing over the phone.

## Exam Tip
Phishing uses deceptive messages and links to trick users into revealing data.
