# L02-Q006

**Difficulty:** Hard
**Domain:** Threat Motivations
**Objective:** 2.3 Explain motivations of threat actors
**Security+ Objective Mapping:** SY0-701 Objective 2.3 - Threat actor motivations

## Scenario
A group defaces a government website and posts a manifesto about climate policy. Which motivation best fits this attack?

## Choices
A. Financial gain
B. Competitor advantage
C. Ideology
D. Accidental disclosure

## Correct Answer
C

## Detailed Explanation
Defacing a website for political protest indicates ideological motivation, typical of hacktivists.

## Why the Incorrect Options Are Wrong
- A: No financial motive is described.
- B: This does not appear to be business competition.
- D: The attack is intentional, not accidental.

## Exam Tip
Hacktivists are often motivated by ideology, social causes, or political statements.
